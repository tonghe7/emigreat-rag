Nordic Council Music Prize

The Nordic Council Music Prize focuses on the performance and creation of music of a high artistic standard. The prize is awarded on alternate years to a piece of music by a living composer and to a small or large ensemble.

    2023

    Winner of the 2023 Nordic Council Music Prize

    The 2023 Nordic Council Music Prize goes to the folk musician, singer-songwriter, and kantele artist Maija Kauhanen from Finland.

    2022

    Winner of the 2022 Nordic Council Music Prize

    The 2022 Nordic Council Music Prize goes to Karin Rehnqvist from Sweden for her work “Silent Earth”.

    2021

    Winner of the 2021 Nordic Council Music Prize

    The 2021 Nordic Council Music Prize goes to Eivør Pálsdóttir from the Faroe Islands.

    2020

    Winner of the 2020 Nordic Council Music Prize

    The 2020 Nordic Council Music Prize goes to Sampo Haapamäki’s Quarter-tone Piano Concerto (2017).

    2019

    Winner of the 2019 Nordic Council Music Prize

    The 2019 Nordic Council Music Prize goes to Gyða Valtýsdóttir from Iceland.

    2018

    Winner of the Nordic Council Music Prize 2018

    The Nordic Council Music Prize 2018 goes to Norway’s Nils Henrik Asheim for his work Muohta.

    2017

    The winner of the Nordic Council Music Prize 2017

    The Finnish conductor Susanna Mälkki has been awarded the Nordic Council Music Prize 2017.

    2016

    Winner of the Nordic Council Music Prize 2016

    The winner of the 2016 Nordic Council Music Prize is Hans Abrahamsen from Denmark for the song cycle “Let Me Tell You”.
