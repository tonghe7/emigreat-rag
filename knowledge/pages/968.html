Commercial transport

The objective of the Swedish Transport Agency is to enable efficient, safe and sustainable road transport services and a transport market characterized by healthy competition.

We are responsible for adopting certain regulations and ensuring that companies, organizations and individuals abide by them. We also issue licenses, permits and authorizations to transport operators and drivers within the road transport sector.

Please contact us for more information about commercial transport.
