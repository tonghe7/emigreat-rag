    Description

    Your guide to Swedish society

    Informationsverige.se is a website run by the County Administrative Boards that contains information about Sweden for asylum seekers and people who have recently been granted a residence permit.

    The website is a free guide to Swedish society in 10 different languages. You can use it on a computer, a tablet or a smartphone.

    Informationsverige.se gives you a broad range of information Swedish society. You can read about Sweden is governed and how you can participate and influence things. You can learn about the Swedish welfare system with its financial support works, and how Swedish health and medical care are organised. There are also pages where you begin practicing Swedish, pages for parents, and a glossary of important words.
