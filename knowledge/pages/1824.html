Accessibility

This website is run by Brottsförebyggande rådet or Brå (The Swedish National Council for Crime Prevention). This page addresses how bra.se meets the criteria set in the law on accessibility to digital public services, known problems on the website and how to contact us to report an accessibility issue.

How accessible is this website?

We are aware that some areas of the website are not accessible. See below for content that is non-accessible.

What to do if you cannot access parts of this website

If you need information on this website in a different format please contact us by emailing webb@bra.se.

Reporting accessibility problems with this website

We’re always looking to improve the accessibility of this website. If you find any problems not listed on this page or think we’re not meeting accessibility requirements, please contact us by emailing webb@bra.se.

Supervision

The Agency for Digital Government is responsible for following up and supervising that public authorities are complying with the law on accessibility to digital public services. If you’re not happy with how we respond to your complaint, contact the Agency.

Technical information about this website’s accessibility

This website is partially compliant with the law on accessibility to digital public services, due to the non-compliances listed below.

Non accessible content

Non-compliance with the accessibility regulations

  • Some of Brå's statistical tables and data base services are not fully accesible.

Content that’s not within the scope of the accessibility regulations

The content listed below is not fully accessible, but is exempt persuant to 9 § in the law on accessibility to digital public services.

  • Some videos on the website don't have captions. This content is exempt from the law because it was published before 23 September, 2020.
  • Some PDF files that were published before 23 September, 2018 are currently not tagged, so people using assistive technologies may not be able to understand some content. This content is exempt from the law because it was published before the law came into effect.

How we tested this website

This website has been tested by Consid to ensure compliance with WCAG 2.1 AA. The last accessibility test was made on 10 September 2020.

Please contact us by emailing webb@bra.se to access the latest audit of this webisite's accessability.
